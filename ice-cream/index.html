    About FrostyScoops 🍨

    Our Story

    FrostyScoops began as a family passion in a small kitchen, where joy came in frozen scoops. What started with just a hand-cranked machine and a love for creamy desserts soon became a buzzing ice cream parlor with a heart.Today, we craft every flavor with the same care and creativity—from nostalgic classics to daring new blends—all using real, honest ingredients. At FrostyScoops, we don’t just serve ice cream—we scoop happiness!

    🥄 Our Ingredients

    Fresh Milk

    Locally sourced from trusted dairies, our milk is creamy, smooth, and rich in flavor—perfect for crafting the foundation of our classic scoops.

    Fresh Berries

    Hand-picked at the peak of ripeness, our strawberries, raspberries, and blueberries bring natural sweetness and vibrant color to every swirl.

    Pure Cocoa

    We use high-quality cocoa to create deep, indulgent chocolate flavors that are a dream for every chocolate lover.

    Vanilla Beans

    Sourced straight from Madagascar, our real vanilla beans add a warm, aromatic essence you can taste in every spoonful.

    Roasted Nuts

    Crunchy almonds, walnuts, and pistachios roasted to golden perfection adding texture and a nutty kick to select creations.

    Coconut Milk

    Smooth, rich, and completely dairy-free, our coconut milk base is a tropical delight, especially loved by our vegan customers.

    🌱 Our Core Values

    Handmade with Love

    Every scoop is lovingly made by hand. No machines, just passionate people creating something special for you special.

    Organic Ingredients

    We prioritize clean and wholesome food—using organic dairy, fruits, and sweeteners for better taste and better health.

    Locally Sourced

    We believe in supporting our community. That’s why we partner with local farms to bring you fresh, seasonal ingredients.
